\begin{abstract}
This report presents a conceptual structural design study of an aircraft wing box intended for portfolio demonstration purposes. The work focuses on realistic structural layout, internal load paths, and manufacturability considerations representative of early-stage aerospace design. A parametric CAD model is developed to support the structural reasoning, while assumptions and limitations are stated explicitly to reflect professional engineering practice.
\end{abstract}
